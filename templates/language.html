Tweets about Tibet around the World

Map of Twitter Data Related to Tibet

Social media is an incredible vehicle for communication across cities, countries and even continents. Different social media platforms have become increasingly popular as a means of sharing culture and beliefs. The map above displays tweets around the world relating to Tibet over the last month. These tweets were aggregated to analyze. Using machine learning these tweets were processed to understand the natural language and use statistical parsers to detect the sentiment of the tweets. In order to detect the sentiment of the tweets, the sequence, pattern and existnence of words was analyzed and compared to an existing database of annotated text that is labled and positive and negative. These tweets are displayed on a map to examine patterns and behavior related to their locations
